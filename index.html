AFL Australia 2024

The Australian Football League (AFL) is the professional competition for Australian rules football. Teams compete for the premiership title, with matches being played on large oval fields. AFL is widely popular in Australia, and teams are spread across the country.

This webpage provides a visual breakdown of the 2024 AFL season performance metrics using various charts. The visualizations include a map showing team stadiums, bar charts comparing key statistics like goals, kicks, and handballs, a line chart for tackles per game, and a bubble chart that compares disposals and tackles. These charts offer a quick way to explore team performance and patterns throughout the season.

This map shows the location of each team's home stadium. Click on the markers to see the team and stadium name.
This bar chart compares the total number of goals scored by each team during the season.
This line chart shows the number of tackles made by each team over the course of several games.
This bubble chart compares disposals, tackles, and goals for each team. The size of the bubble represents the number of goals.
